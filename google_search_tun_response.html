tun
/tʌn/
noun
noun: tun; plural noun: tuns; noun: tun shell; plural noun: tun shells
  1. 1.
    a large beer or wine cask.
  2. 2.
    an imperial measure of capacity, equal to 4 hogsheads.
verb
archaic
verb: tun; 3rd person present: tuns; past tense: tunned; past participle: tunned; gerund or present participle: tunning
  1. store (wine or other alcoholic drinks) in a tun.
Tip
Similar-sounding words
